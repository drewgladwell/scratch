This past summer I was able to travel to many parts of Europe. These include:



Bacharach, Germany

Small German town just on the Rhine River. Includes:
  • Beautiful views of the river and surrounding hills
  • Many vineyards and wineries
  • Charming old town with cobblestone streets and half-timbered houses


Castle Combe, United Kingdom

Small English village in the Cotswolds. Includes:
  • Picturesque village with charming cottages and a medieval church
  • Beautiful countryside with rolling hills and scenic walking trails
  • Rich history and culture, with many historic buildings and landmarks to explore


Mallorca, Spain

Beautiful island in the Mediterranean Sea. Includes:
  • Stunning beaches with crystal-clear waters and picturesque coves
  • Beautiful countryside with rolling hills, charming villages, and scenic hiking trails
  • Rich history and culture, with many historic buildings and landmarks to explore


Grindelwald, Switzerland

Picturesque mountain village in the Swiss Alps. Includes:
  • Stunning views of the surrounding mountains and glaciers
  • Many outdoor activities, including hiking, skiing, and paragliding
  • Charming village with traditional Swiss architecture and culture
